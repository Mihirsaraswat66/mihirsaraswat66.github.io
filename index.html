About Mihir

Mihir Saraswat is a youth leader from Giridih, Jharkhand. He currently serves as the District Council Coordinator (2025–26) of Rotaract District 3250. He co-owns MM Medicine and MM Ayurveda, working at the intersection of entrepreneurship and grassroots service. His journey in Rotaract began as Club Secretary of the Rotaract Club of Giridih Shakti, and his leadership has grown across multiple district roles over the years.
