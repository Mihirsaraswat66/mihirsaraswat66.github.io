About Me

Born on April 26, 1997, in Giridih, Jharkhand, I am a passionate Rotaract leader and social entrepreneur. I’ve held leadership roles such as Club Secretary of Rotaract Club of Giridih Shakti (2021–23), Club Vice President of Rotaract Club of Amrapali (2023–25), and various District posts. I co-own MM Medicine and MM Ayurveda, focused on accessible community wellness and healthcare. My mission is to empower youth and communities through action, awareness, and entrepreneurship.
