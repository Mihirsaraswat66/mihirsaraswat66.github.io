About

Born on 26 April 1997 in Giridih, Jharkhand, Mihir Saraswat is a youth leader, entrepreneur, and passionate community builder. He is the co-owner of MM Medicine & MM Ayurveda. Currently, he serves as the District Council Coordinator (2025–26) of Rotaract District 3250.

Rotaract Journey

  • Club Secretary, Rotaract Club of Giridih Shakti (2021–23)
  • Club Vice President, Rotaract Club of Amrapali (2023–25)
  • District PR Team Member (2022–24)
  • District Club Service Director (2023–24)
  • District Sergeant-at-Arms (2024–25)
  • District Council Coordinator (2025–26)

Awards

  • Most Supporting Rotaractor (2023–24)
  • Outstanding PR Member (2023–24)
  • Best Director of the Year
