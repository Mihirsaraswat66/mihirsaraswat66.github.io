About Mihir

Born 26 April 1997 in Giridih, Jharkhand, Mihir Saraswat is a youth leader, social entrepreneur and community organiser. He currently serves as District Council Coordinator (2025–26) of Rotaract District 3250, and is co-owner of MM Medicine & MM Ayurveda. Mihir has held several district and club roles and won multiple awards for service and PR leadership.
